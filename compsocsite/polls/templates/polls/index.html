Customized Singular Polls

OPRA allows you to customize your poll by choosing question types (Poll or Allocation), voting algorithms and visibility. Also, OPRA allows anonymous voters to join singular polls. You must set a question title (keep it short and concise). You can also add a description if you need to add more information. It will be shown when you hover over the title in the index page.

Organized & Analyzed Results

OPRA helps you make better decisions. After a poll ends, OPRA will compute and analyze, using powerful voting algorithms, the results for you.
